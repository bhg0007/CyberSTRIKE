Why is Cyber Hygiene Important?

Cyber hygiene helps protect your personal information, reduce the risk of cyber threats, and ensure safe online practices. It is essential for your digital well-being and long-term safety online.

Social Media Behaviors to Avoid

Examples of risky behaviors include:

  • Oversharing personal information (e.g., address, school name)
  • Accepting friend requests from strangers
  • Clicking suspicious links or downloads
  • Using weak or repeated passwords
